The Simple 5-Step Journey

How Going Live Works

The whole process takes about 60 days, and RHH handles nearly all of it. Tap any step to see exactly what happens and who does what.

Tap a step to expand

RHH DOES

  • Host a call to align on goals
  • Introduce the Rural Health Hub platform
  • Understand your current workflows
  • Review your Telehealth Suite equipment

YOUR CLINIC DOES

  • Share your workflows
  • Name your staff point person
  • Determine Go Live date
Your effort1-hour meeting

RHH DOES

  • Configure the platform for your clinic
  • Create staff accounts and access
  • Work with IT department as needed to configure equipment
  • Share workflow and IT documents

YOUR CLINIC DOES

  • Confirm staff list
  • Install Telehealth Equipment and give IT permissions
Your effortAccept platform invites & set up your telehealth suite

RHH DOES

  • Build your clinic page so specialists can see your local resources
  • Sit down with your staff to map where remote providers should order labs, radiology, prescriptions, and more
  • Connect your clinic into the specialty network
  • Confirm and test your information and access is correct and live
  • Set training date

YOUR CLINIC DOES

  • Provide information on local medical care
  • Provide your preferred regional medical networks
  • Add RHH providers to your 340B list
Your effortMeet with RHH staff for 1–2 hours

RHH DOES

  • Deliver comprehensive training sessions
  • Review quick-reference guides
  • Answer questions
  • Provide direct phone numbers for RHH support team
  • Show your billing team how to bill Origination Site fee

YOUR CLINIC DOES

  • Clinic staff attends one or two short sessions
  • Learn and practice the simple workflows
Your effortAbout one hour of training — or as much as you need

RHH DOES

  • Stand by for live support on day one
  • Monitor early consults and check in

YOUR CLINIC DOES

  • Begin curbside consults & telehealth referrals with patients
Your effortStart referring patients
